# INAYA Domestic Workers Website Design System

This is the locked design direction for the website.

## Mood

Dubai premium domestic service: clean, trustworthy, luxurious, airy, and Apple-inspired.

## Core Principles

- Background is soft warm ivory / off-white, never pure white as the main page canvas.
- Primary brand color is the INAYA logo deep navy.
- Gold is used carefully as a refined accent, not as a bright yellow theme.
- Cards use glassmorphism: light blur, thin navy border, translucent white, and soft shadow.
- Layouts must feel airy, aligned, and calm, not crowded.
- Buttons use pill shapes with navy/gold styling and premium hover states.
- Icons are thin line icons in navy/gold. Original-color social icons are allowed only in the floating social bar.
- Images should be real lifestyle/home-service photography or clean office/location visuals, not heavy AI collage.
- Arabic layouts mirror direction, but phone numbers and technical values must remain controlled and readable.
- UAE-wide service coverage should remain clear without over-repeating it in every block.

## Components

- Navbar: translucent glass on ivory, thin border, compact height, navy text, navy pill booking button.
- Cards: `20px` radius, `rgba(255,255,255,0.72)`, thin navy border, blur, soft shadow.
- Buttons: primary navy pill, secondary glass pill, gold outline pill, WhatsApp green pill only for WhatsApp.
- Forms: glass or ivory fields, thin border, navy focus ring, clear labels.
- Map/location panels: light glass panels with navy/gold location accents.

## Spacing

Use an 8px system:

`8, 12, 16, 24, 40, 64`

Sections should have generous vertical spacing and clean visual separation.

## Page Rules

- One clear visual direction per page.
- Avoid repeating contact blocks in multiple places.
- Do not mix many accent colors.
- No nested cards unless unavoidable.
- Keep hero sections calm and premium.
- Keep content real and specific to INAYA Domestic Workers across the UAE.
